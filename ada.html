Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

This statement describes our commitment to digital accessibility and our ongoing efforts to conform to WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards.

Section 1

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Flow & Finish is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure our holistic design portfolio, space strategy frameworks, and digital interfaces are welcoming and usable for all visitors.

Section 2

Conformance Status

We actively work to align this website with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA — the current W3C standard, which also encompasses the earlier 2.1 success criteria. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive disabilities.

This site is substantially conformant with WCAG 2.2 Level AA, meaning most content meets the standard while some elements may not yet fully conform. We treat accessibility as an ongoing process and welcome reports of any barriers. In pursuing these benchmarks we also have regard to the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Section 508 in the United States, the European Accessibility Act and EN 301 549 in the European Union, the Equality Act 2010 in the United Kingdom, and the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA) and the Accessible Canada Act.

Section 3

Core Accessibility Features Implemented on Our Site

To optimize navigation and usability, this site is built using the following structural frameworks:

  • Semantic Code: Clean HTML structure and semantic tags to ensure assistive technologies (like screen readers) can accurately parse and read content.
  • Contrast & Typography: Strict attention to text-to-background contrast ratios and elegant, legible typography to minimize cognitive load and visual fatigue.
  • Alternative Text: Inclusion of descriptive alt text for images, portfolios, structural transformations, and secondary luxury resale market archival imagery.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Clean element sequencing to support users navigating without a mouse.

Section 4

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of Flow & Finish relies on the following technologies to work seamlessly with web browsers and popular assistive technologies or screen readers:

  • HTML
  • CSS

These core layers are fundamentally relied upon to meet and maintain our WCAG 2.2 Level AA conformance standards.

Section 5

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our platform. If you experience any accessibility barriers or layout issues while navigating our site, please let us know immediately so we can remedy it:

  • Email: beflowandfinish@gmail.com

We take all reports seriously. Our team actively reviews all accessibility feedback and aims to respond and implement necessary design remediations within 3 business days.
