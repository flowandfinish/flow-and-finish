Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Flow & Finish, we believe that exceptional spatial design should be rooted in natural simplicity, clarity, and universal ease of use. We are deeply committed to ensuring that our digital experience is fully accessible and welcoming to all individuals, regardless of physical, sensory, or cognitive ability.

We are actively working to align our website configuration with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA standards. These international guidelines establish clear criteria for improving web accessibility for individuals with disabilities, and satisfying them helps us ensure our platform is easily navigable around the world.

Actions and Accomplishments

To ensure a robust, inclusive user experience, our website integration incorporates the following core accessibility measures:

  • Semantic Structure: We utilize clean, hierarchical HTML structural formatting to ensure assistive technologies and screen readers can easily parse, scan, and interpret our content sequence.
  • Text Alternatives: We proactively implement descriptive Alternative Text (Alt Text) for all essential portfolio imagery, project highlights, and graphic elements, conveying visual texture and layouts to low-vision or blind visitors.
  • Color Contrast Configuration: Our brand color palette text displays are evaluated to meet or exceed the required 4.5:1 contrast ratio, guaranteeing optimal text legibility across varying lighting and device conditions.
  • Keyboard Navigation Compatibility: Our site elements are engineered to be entirely operable without a mouse. Users can tab through menus, open or close dialog boxes, and fill out our contact and inquiry fields utilizing a standard keyboard layout.
  • Global Technical Foundation: We integrate proper language attributes within our code framework to allow seamless machine translation and local screen-reader pronunciation across international boundaries.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines define three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Flow & Finish is currently partially conformant with WCAG 2.2 Level AA. "Partially conformant" means that while the vast majority of our core content, brand messaging, and contact systems fully satisfy the accessibility criteria, certain specialized components or older media elements may occasionally undergo adjustments to optimize their interface.

Known Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our rigorous efforts to ensure total digital accessibility across our platform, a highly visual interior design and space planning portfolio may occasionally present minor structural challenges.

  • Third-Party Scripts & Integrations: Our website utilizes specific third-party integrations (such as interactive maps for office navigation, real-time font rendering systems, or social media portfolio feeds). While we encourage all partners to provide fully compliant plugins, these external frames remain outside of our direct operational modification control.
  • Archival Documentation: Older downloadable project files or historical blueprint diagrams may lack full text alternatives.

If you encounter an element that presents a barrier, please contact us directly, and we will happily provide a fully accessible, manual alternative version of that asset immediately.
